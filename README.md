# Solo Sessions

A private, personal pleasure tracker. Runs entirely in your browser — no server, no accounts, no data ever leaves your device.

## Features

- Log masturbation sessions with date, time, duration, orgasm strength, mood, stimulus, position, location, edging, toy use, and arse play
- Analytics tab with 8 charts: monthly sessions, orgasm strength trends, day of week, time of day, mood breakdown, stimulus breakdown, favourite positions and locations
- Summary stats: total sessions, average duration, average per week, longest streak, edging/toy/arse play rates
- Full session history with tags and delete
- Works offline after first load
- Installs to your homescreen as an app

## Data & Privacy

All data is stored in your browser's `localStorage`. Nothing is sent anywhere. If you clear your browser data, your sessions will be lost — so don't do that.

## Setup (GitHub Pages)

1. Go to your repo **Settings → Pages**
2. Source: **Deploy from a branch**
3. Branch: **main**, folder: **/ (root)**
4. Save — your app will be live at `https://Chunky-Git-33.github.io/Solo-Sessions/`

## Install to Homescreen (Chrome on Android)

1. Open the app URL in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮)
3. Tap **Add to Home screen**
4. Tap **Add**

It will appear on your homescreen and open fullscreen like a native app.

## Install to Homescreen (Safari on iPhone)

1. Open the app URL in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**
